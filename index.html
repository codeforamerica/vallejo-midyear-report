Problem Statement

“Engage residents to improve the public safety of residents and transform the relationship between the community and the police department.”

Our city partner expressed several concerns about strained relations between Vallejo residents and the city’s police department. On the one hand, our city partner cited a string of recent officer-involved shootings that spurred protests at City Council meetings; on the other hand, he also worried that residents were unsatisfied with the department’s limited resources to investigate property crimes, which occur with frequency in Vallejo.

More specifically, our city partner mentioned ways to increase trust between police and residents by performing the following:

  • Rebuild the trust between the community and government Prevent a Ferguson Get to the youth Reduce crime Increase quality of life Improve processes Build off of neighborhood watch program Proactive engagement Predictive Analytics Give people a voice Involve the Clergy Positive press
